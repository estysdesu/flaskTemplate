About

Apex Cabinetry has been in business since 1997 and our sales have increased significantly each year. In 2004 we provided and installed more than 2 million dollars worth of product.

Our scope of work include commerical casework, millwork, store fixtures, solid surface, phenolic resin labatory tops, and granite. We also have extensive experience in unique finishing techniques, such as; touch-up, custom matching any stain color,poured on finishes and marbleizing.

Delivery and installation is available within a 150 mile range of Cincinnati with nationwide shipping. Our cabinetmakers have from 4 to 22 years experience working in all areas of woodworking industry.
